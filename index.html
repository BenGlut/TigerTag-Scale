TigerTagScale – ESP32 Web Flasher

Flash the firmware to your ESP32 directly from your browser.

Requirements: Use Google Chrome / Microsoft Edge on desktop. Plug the ESP32 via USB. If nothing appears, install the USB driver for your board (CP210x/CH340).

If the button is disabled, your browser may not support Web Serial. Try Chrome/Edge on Windows, macOS, or Linux.
