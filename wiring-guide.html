Important Notes
  • Always use 3.3V for all components - ESP32 pins are NOT 5V tolerant
  • Double-check all connections before powering on
  • Use quality jumper wires to avoid intermittent connections
  • Keep wires as short as possible to reduce interference
  • The load cell colors may vary by manufacturer - check datasheet
